    • Pretty_Okc

    The Pretty Okc plugin provides cleaner, consistent, and flexible styling on the main pages of OkCupid, as well as bringing back several old features that Okc has removed, and adding a couple of new ones.

    • Multiple, custom favorites lists.
    • Multiple ways to view Match Results, including Classic Mode.
    • Add private notes to people from Profiles, Favorites, Likes, Visitors Pages.
    • Unread Messages Indicator on Browser Icon.
    • Brings back “Recently Answered” to Questions page.
    • Cleaner UX on profiles, favorites lists, and other pages.

    To install, simply download the extension and drag it onto chrome://extensions.
